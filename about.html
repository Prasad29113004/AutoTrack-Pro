About the project

Transparent service builds lasting trust.

AutoTrack Pro connects customers and service teams through clear, timely information.

Our purpose

No more wondering what is happening in the workshop.

Vehicle owners often depend on calls and verbal updates while their vehicle is being serviced. AutoTrack Pro replaces that uncertainty with a structured digital timeline, work photographs, clear cost breakdowns, and scheduled handover information.

The system is deliberately built with beginner-friendly HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. It demonstrates authentication, role-based interfaces, CRUD operations, visual tracking, local persistence, and responsive UI design without a framework.

Project values

Designed around three simple ideas

◉

Transparency

Customers can see what has been completed, when it happened, and what it cost.

↗

Communication

Dashboard notifications and notes create a dependable service update channel.

✓

Accountability

Photos, itemised parts, and a quality-check timeline document completed work.
